I am a PhD student advised by Dr. Anupam Das, at North Carolina State University. My research interests are in all things Security and Privacy with a focus on IoT and Web Security. I am also a member of the Wolfpack Security and Privacy Lab (WSPR). Previously, I graduated from BS Computer Science from Lahore University of Management Sciences class of 2020.

Research Interests

IoT Security and Privacy

The focus of my research is to make IoT as a platform more secure. IoT is not the future any more it is as much present as anything and security and privacy aspect of it has been understated. The impact billions of insecure and vulnerable devices can have is huge. Through my research I want to make the platform more secure and privacy preserving.

Web Security

Web is the primary component which connects millions of people worldwide and enables interactions through various other platforms. To ensure security and privacy in web and its applications are of paramount importance and a passion of mine. My research work focuses on preventing bad actors from doing bad things and giving control back to the users
